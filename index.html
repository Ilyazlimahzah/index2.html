a Little something About myself

I’m a writer. Well, writer by hobby and not profession. Not anymore at least. I mostly do poetry, which is why I say it’s more of a hobby. If you ask me though, I could tell you a million different reasons why I write and none of them would be “because it’s my hobby” or “because I enjoy it”. Anyways, I think of myself more of a poet than a writer. It’s as I say, “every poet is a writer but not every writer is a poet”. Writing stories and whatnot is one thing, poetry on the other hand comes from somewhere different. One could write stories to entertain regardless of the veracity of what’s in the writing, it could be informing, educative, it could be a number of things depending on what the writer is going for. Which is why this could easily be a profession. It comes with returns. Poetry only speaks to a certain audience, and one can either resonate with it or not. It’s as simple as that.

I’m a part time photographer and videographer as well. That, I only just became fond of recently. It’s been months now, and nothing short of amazing. I can tell it’s going to be a long-term deal. I would say photography summarizes my love for art in general in a canvas; makes it still. Capturing moments, reliving captured moments. Aubrey Drake once said, “some nights I wish I could go back in time, not to change shit but to feel a couple things twice”. Read that again. How best do you feel things all over again if not through memories which could easily be pictures and videos. You could be basking in just as much euphoria reliving the day, this time, with no interference from the universe itself. It plays out just how you remember it. Just how you remember it. Rest in that.

I work for the company “theInfoNerds”. I’ve since come to learn a lot, met good people, made alliances that have been awesome thus far. It goes without saying the job takes me around, different locations of course which is thrilling because I do enjoy exploring new sceneries. The experience in general has been fantastic.

I also play basketball and I work out. Notice how these came last? Well, I’ll also be saying the least about them because they very much speak for themselves.
